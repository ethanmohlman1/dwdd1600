Submit a Rock

Do you have a Rock you'd like to see me review? Send it to me. It can be something you love, something you hate, or something you know nothing about. I will review everything that is sent (within reason), because if I found everything myself the majority would be stealth games and murder mysteries.

Submission criteria

  1. The work must be fairly obscure. This is a very vague criteria, so use your best judgement.
  2. The work must have mixed or neutral scores. If the work has online reviews and/or critic scores, they cannot be overwhelmingly positive or negative. Think in the 4-7/10 range.
  3. The work can be any kind of media, as long as it exists in digital form. Make sure its easily accessible online. If it takes some digging, a (legal) link would be appreciated.
  4. No fanworks/works from small independent teams! While I try to remain positive in my reviews, it's preferable if I avoid critiquing non-professionals and their passion projects without permission.
